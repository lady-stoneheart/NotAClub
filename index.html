Hadith Library

Timeless wisdom, graded and explained.

Actions are by Intentions

Bukhari & Muslim

SAHIH
"The reward of deeds depends upon the intentions and every person will get the reward according to what he has intended."
Explanation: This is considered one of the most fundamental principles in Islam. It emphasizes that the physical action is secondary to the condition of the heart. A small deed can become great due to intention, and a great deed can become worthless due to lack of sincerity.

Good Character

Jami At-Tirmidhi

SAHIH
"The heaviest thing to be placed in the balance of a believing slave on the Day of Judgment will be good character."
Explanation: This highlights the weight of social conduct. While ritual worship is vital, how one treats others—family, neighbors, and strangers—carries immense weight in the final accountability.

Leaving What Doesn't Concern You

Sunan Ibn Majah

HASAN
"Part of the perfection of one’s Islam is his leaving that which does not concern him."
Explanation: A key to productivity and peace of mind. Focusing on one's own responsibilities, duties, and spiritual growth rather than interfering in the affairs of others or consuming useless information leads to spiritual excellence (Ihsan).

What does "BAMI" mean?

The story behind the name

GENERAL

BAMI stands for Beyond A Mere Idea.

Too many projects die in the "idea phase." This acronym serves as a constant reminder that execution is the only thing that matters. Code over concepts.

Why Vanilla JavaScript?

Technical decisions

TECH

Modern web development often over-engineers simple problems. By sticking to Vanilla JS, HTML, and CSS, we ensure:

  • Zero build steps
  • Instant load times
  • Long-term maintainability
  • Easy understanding for beginners

Is this Open Source?

Licensing & Contribution

LEGAL

The Origin Story

February 2026

GENESIS

It started with a cluttered screen. The digital landscape had become too loud—notifications fighting for attention, algorithms deciding what we see, and complexity masquerading as progress.

I wanted a quiet corner. Not a social network, not a club, but a signal in the noise. Project BAMI was born from a desire to strip away the non-essential. To build something that respects the user's intelligence and time.
